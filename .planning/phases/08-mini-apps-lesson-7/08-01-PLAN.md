## Simulated AI Strategy

**Why simulation, not real API?**:
1. **Cost**: No API fees for school deployment
2. **Privacy**: No data sent to external servers (DSGVO-compliant)
3. **Reliability**: Works offline, no rate limits
4. **Safety**: No unexpected/inappropriate responses
5. **Pedagogical**: Children learn AI concepts without needing actual AI

**How to maintain "AI magic" illusion**:
- Loading animations (simulate processing time)
- Variety in responses (25 stories, 20 jokes, 50 names)
- Contextual reactions from Kiki ("Wow, das ist intelligent!")
- No obvious patterns (randomization where possible)
- Language suggests intelligence ("KI denkt sich aus...")

**Teaching moment** (Phase 9 Teacher Mode):
- Teachers can explain: "This simulates how real AI works"
- Honest approach: "Real AI has millions of stories, we have 25"
- Focus on concepts, not specific technology
